Core Rules

The core mechanic of BareBones D12 Edition revolves around rolling a d12 and adding the relevant attribute modifier. The result is then compared to the difficulty rating of the test for social encounter or combat action.

  • A natural 12 on the d12 is an automatic success, even without adding any modifiers. Conversely, **a natural 1** is a **fumble**.
  • When a fumble occurs, the referee inflicts a hard move on the player character.

Any type of roll done during game is called a Test. Every Test is tied to an attribute.

The referee sets a Difficulty Rating (DR) that the player must meet or exceed with their roll.
